YetiNova: Master Content & Design Specification
Version 2.0 (The Accelerator Model)
Global Design Standards
Typography: Inter / Geist / Sans-serif.
Tone: High-Signal, Urgent, Professional. Avoid corporate fluff. Use active verbs ("Build," "Launch," "Scale").
Visual Motif: "The Ascent." Use the word ASCEND as a large, faint watermark in background sections.
Visual Hierarchy: Contrast between "Raw/Gritty" (for builders) and "Clean/Trustworthy" (for investors).

3. OUR MODEL (The Methodology)
Design Concept: 2x2 Grid (The "Engine").
Intro: "We don't do classrooms. We do product sprints. A rigorous four-stage framework to turn ideas into assets."
Grid Item 01: Selection
Scouting the Bold
We identify high-potential founders with unique insights. We don't fund business plans; we fund obsession and grit.
Grid Item 02: The Sprint
Build & Break
Rapid prototyping. We transform concepts into MVPs with robust architecture. If it doesn't work, we kill it fast. If it works, we double down.
Grid Item 03: The Launch
Market Reality
Governance from Day 1. We structure the venture, handle the legal compliance, and push the product to real users.
Grid Item 04: Scale
Global Reach
Preparing the startup for national expansion and export readiness. We connect founders to the capital needed to grow.
